Gin & Tonic


Equipment
  • 1 Jigger
  • 1 Bar spoon

Ingredients
  • 2 ounces Gin
  • 6 ounces Tonic water
  • 1 lemon/lime wheel

Instructions
  1. Fill your glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add a butof tonic water, approx 1 - 1.5 oz. 6 oz Tonic water.
  3. Gently pur in the Gin followed by the rest of the tonoc water. 2 oz Gin. 6 oz Tonic water.
  4. Press some lemon juice into the drink and add your garnish to the drink.

Asperol Gin & Tonic


Equipment
  • 1 Jigger

Ingredients
  • 1.5 oz Gin (Edinburgh Seaside Gin or Gin Mare)
  • 0.5 oz Asperol
  • 4.5 oz Mediterranean tonic water
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • 1 dash Orange bitters

Instructions
  1. Add ice spheres in a balloon or wine glass and swirl to chill it.
  2. Get rid of melted water and then pour in the Gin.
  3. Add Asperol, give it a quick stir and then fill up with tonic water.
  4. Add one dash of Angostura bitters and one dash of orange bitters.
  5. Garnish with a slice of orange.

Pink Fig & Tonic


Equipment
  • 1 Bar spoon
  • 1 Jigger

Ingredients
  • 2 oz London Dry Gin
  • 4 oz Tonic water (Indian or dry)
  • 0.5 oz Fig Syrup
  • 0.25 oz lemon juice
  • 2 slices fresh fig (for garnish)

Instructions
  1. Add Gin, fig syrup and a splash of lemon juice to your highball glass. Give everything a good stir until Gin and syrup are blended fully
  2. Now add a large ice cube and top up everything with tonic water. (4 oz of Tonic Water)
  3. Use two slices of fresh fig to garnish your drink. Cheers!. (2 slices of fresh fig)
