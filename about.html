The GranTonic Cocktail Bar


A neighborhood bar at heart, we opened our doors in 2013 and never looked back. Through our eleven years we have constantly been ranked as one of the world's best bars and received countless awards for both our menus, our bartenders and our atmosphere.


We focus on serving exceptional classic gin & tonic cocktails, with our characteristic twist of flavors and fragrances from southern Europe, northern Africa, and the Middle East. The Mediterranean region has always been a big part of our world and continues to influence our cocktails, as well as our food and wine.


Music and culture has been at the heart of GranTonic since the very beginning, and remains a vital part of everything we do. As well as constantly featuring some of the best local DJs, we also host fashion parties, art exhibitions and special events. Our vibrant cocktail bar is a great spot to start an evening, and an excellent place to stay all night.


So why not stop by for a cocktail or two, and enjoy our exquisite facilities!
